Instructions For Typing speed Game.

-you have 3 Levels in this Game.

-Every Level has Different Words.

-The Easy Level Has small words..each word has 5 seconds to write.

-The Normal Level Has intermediate words..each word has 4 seconds to write.

-The Hard Level Has long and complex words..each word has 3 seconds to write.

How to start?

- you have to select level first.

- after that the game will appears

- to begin press start playing

- at the end of the box you will see your score and your time left

- you have to type each word before the time end or you will lose
